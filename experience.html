RESEARCH EXPERIENCE

Development of Instrument on Robot-aided, Cognitive Virtual Rehabilitation for Automatic Physical Training of Individuals with Disabilities (iRAPID)
Jan. 2014 - Dec. 2015
  • Designed the system Digital Gloves with a 3D blooming flower for stroke rehabilitation. The digital gloves included five bending sensors and three dimensional motion sensors. The game designed was using OpenGL graphical software. The upper-body motion analysis algorithm involves gesture recognition and finger movement analysis
  • Built the system Digital Gloves with the Virtual Piano for stroke rehabilitation. The algorithm to determine notes was inspired by step-counting algorithms designed for SmartShoe sensors. The system was validated by non-piano players and myself as a twenty-years experienced piano player.
  • Performed a study for daily activity analysis with the SmartSocks. The SmartSocks was designed with bending sensors and gyroscopes. The activity analysis algorithm involves Support Vector Machines.
Longitudinal Assessment of Risk of Falling
Sep. 2011 - Dec. 2013
  • Created a cyber-physical system platform with SmartShoe sensors, android applications and the remote servers to wirelessly monitor lower-body activities and provide biofeedback. The system involves ubiquitous computing with algorithms running in sensors, mobile phones and servers.
  • Developed and compared several pattern recognition methods, such as Decision Trees, Support Vector Machines and Neural Networks for daily activity classification, including sitting, standing, walking, bicycling and climbing stairs.
  • Extended the study utilizing the SmartShoes and the platform I had built for free living population of stroke patients and children with Cerebral palsy.
